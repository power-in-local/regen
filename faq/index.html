Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about ReGen Chiropractic, our treatments, and what to expect at your first visit.

How is ReGen different from a regular chiropractor?+
Most chiropractic offices offer adjustments only. ReGen combines seven treatment modalities: chiropractic adjustments, trigger point injection therapy, Wharton's Jelly biologics, exosome therapy, FDA-cleared Class IV cold laser therapy, DME knee bracing, and a regenerative supplement protocol — all coordinated into one personalized plan.
Can you help me avoid knee replacement surgery?+
Many patients come to ReGen after being told knee replacement is their only option. Our regenerative protocol has helped many patients significantly reduce or eliminate knee pain without surgery. The earlier you start, the more options you have.
What is the difference between Wharton's Jelly biologics and PRP?+
Traditional PRP is made from spinning your own blood in-office — results vary based on your platelet count, age, and health. Wharton's Jelly biologics are derived from umbilical cord tissue and contain growth factors at a standardized, concentrated level. Results are more consistent and predictable.
What is exosome therapy?+
Cord blood-derived exosomes are nano-sized messengers that carry growth factors, proteins, and RNA to signal the body's own repair mechanisms. They contain PDGF, TGF-β1, VEGF, and IL-1RA — working at the cellular level to reduce inflammation and support tissue regeneration.
How does cold laser therapy work?+
Class IV cold laser therapy uses red and near-infrared light wavelengths that penetrate deep into tissue — reducing inflammation, improving circulation, decreasing pain signals, and supporting the body's natural repair process. FDA-cleared, drug-free, needle-free, no downtime.
Is cold laser the same as red light therapy?+
They use similar technology but are not the same. Consumer red light devices operate at much lower power and penetrate only a few millimeters into tissue. Class IV therapeutic lasers operate at significantly higher power and penetrate much deeper — reaching joints, tendons, and deep muscle tissue.
What is trigger point injection therapy?+
Massage works on muscle knots from the outside. Trigger point injection therapy works from the inside — a small injection placed directly into the contracted muscle knot releases the fibers at the source. This produces faster and longer-lasting results, especially for chronic tightness that hasn't responded to massage or stretching.
Do you accept insurance?+
ReGen Chiropractic operates on a cash-based model. This allows Dr. Andrew to recommend exactly what your condition needs — not what insurance approves. Many of the most effective treatments including biologics and laser therapy are not covered by insurance. All costs are discussed openly during your free consultation.
Is there a free consultation?+
Yes — always. Your free consultation is a real conversation with Dr. Andrew, not a sales pitch. He reviews your condition, history, what you've already tried, and your goals, then gives you an honest assessment of whether ReGen is a good fit.
Who is a good candidate for treatment at ReGen?+
Most of our patients are active adults between 45 and 75 — golfers, pickleball and tennis players, walkers, dancers — dealing with knee pain, shoulder pain, neck pain, or back pain. They've often tried cortisone, PT, or standard chiropractic without lasting results.
How many visits will I need?+
Every plan is different depending on your condition, which treatments are included, and how your body responds. Dr. Andrew outlines a clear care plan with realistic expectations and a treatment timeline during your free consultation.
Do you treat whiplash from car accidents?+
Yes. Whiplash involves both joint dysfunction and soft tissue injury. The ReGen neck protocol addresses both: decompression and adjustment for the structural component, EMS, ultrasound, and trigger point therapy for the soft tissue damage.

Dr. Andrew will review your condition, history, and goals — then build a personalized plan. No pressure. No commitment.
